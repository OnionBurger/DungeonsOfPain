arrows or WASD - move
spacebar - wait turn
ctrl - pickup item/use stairs
left click: on tile - inspect item or enemy, on item in inventory - use item (then left click to target if needed)
shift + left click - inspect item in inventory
nums 1 to 9 and 0 - left click item in inventory (can be combined with shift to inspect)
right click - unequip or drop item
escape - quit

note for modding: when specifying duration for buffs in steps, type one extra, cuz step when applying buff counts as the first
